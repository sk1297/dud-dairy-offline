Privacy Policy — Dud Dairy App

Last updated: May 2025

1. Overview

Dud Dairy ("the App") is a dairy management application for Android devices. This privacy policy explains how we handle information within the App.

2. Data Collection

The App does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data entered in the App (customer names, delivery records, payments, bills) is stored only on your device in a local SQLite database. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party at any time.

3. Data Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Customer names, mobile numbers, and addresses
  • Delivery records and quantities
  • Payment records
  • Monthly bill summaries
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share it using the built-in backup/export feature.

4. Internet Access

The App does not require internet access to function. It works fully offline. The only time the App may access the internet is when you share a bill PDF via WhatsApp or another app — in that case, the sharing is handled entirely by that third-party app (WhatsApp, Gmail, etc.) and is subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • Storage / File access — to save and read the local database and exported PDF files

No other permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts) are requested.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. It is a business tool for dairy operators.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
